We put people at the center of everything we do


ARBOOX™ is an augmented reality app made for everyone. This means that accessibility is present at all times, from the design to the development and the production. We always think about our users' needs to shape the look and feel of the app.



Around the globe we go


At ARBOOX™, we're open to meeting new people, new cultures and living the experiences. That's why, we've set the goal to open physical stores and galleries by 2025 worldwide. Our first store will be located at the heart of Nuevo Polanco, Mexico City.
